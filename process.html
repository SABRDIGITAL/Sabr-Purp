A simple, done-for-you process

We keep everything clear and structured so you always know what’s happening, without getting buried in technical details.

Four straightforward steps from first message to live site.

1

Quick discovery chat

You tell us what you do, who your customers are and what isn’t working at the moment (if you already have a site or socials).

For you: a normal conversation, not a sales pitch. We’ll be honest if you don’t need anything complicated.

2

Layout & content plan

We sketch a simple structure for your site: which pages you need, what goes on each one and what content we’ll use or ask from you.

For you: no guessing or staring at empty boxes. We’ll guide what to send and keep it as light as possible.

3

Design & build

We design your site in our clean, futuristic style or adapt to your existing branding, then build it with performance and clarity in mind.

For you: you get a working preview before anything goes live and a chance to give feedback.

4

Launch & support

We connect your domain, handle the technical side and make sure everything works properly on mobile and desktop.

For you: a live site, a clear handover and the option for us to keep looking after updates if you don’t want to touch the tech.

If you’ve never had a website before, or had a bad experience last time, we’ll guide you through calmly step by step.
