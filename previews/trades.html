FAQ

Questions people usually ask before they book.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

+

We’ll tell you upfront if there’s a call-out and what it covers. For local leak and blockage jobs we usually roll it into the first hour on site, so you’re not paying twice.

Are you insured and Gas Safe registered?

+

Yes. Our engineers are Gas Safe registered for boiler work and we carry full public liability insurance. Certificates are provided after every relevant job.

How soon can you usually come out?

+

For emergencies, we try to attend the same day where possible. For boiler swaps and bathroom refits, we book a clear start date and realistic schedule so you can plan around it.
