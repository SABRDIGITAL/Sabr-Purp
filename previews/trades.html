FAQ

Questions people usually ask first.

Do you charge call-out fees?

+

We’ll tell you upfront if there’s a call-out charge and what it covers. For most local jobs, we roll this into the first hour of work so you’re not paying twice.

Are you insured and certified for boiler work?

+

Yes. Our engineers are Gas Safe registered, and we carry full public liability insurance. Boiler certificates are sent after every applicable job.

How quickly can you usually get to us?

+

For emergencies, we try to attend the same day where possible. For planned work like boiler swaps or bathroom refits, we book a clear start date with you.
