Style 01 – Clean modern

Hero section

Clear, simple headline, one-line explainer and one main button. Minimal background detail so the text is easy to read.

Services section

Three or four cards explaining what you do, who it’s for and what people can expect.

Gallery / proof

Simple grid of your best photos with space for short captions where needed.

Contact & bookings

Clear “next step” – call, message, book or fill in a form. Easy to use on mobile.

Style 02 – Bold futuristic

Hero section

Gradient-heavy hero with layered shapes and punchy wording for bolder brands.

Feature highlights

Icon-led blocks pulling out the most important benefits, not just features.

Social proof

Testimonials, stats and logos placed where they actually help people decide.

Style 03 – Warm & human

Story-led intro

Hero copy that sounds like a human speaking, not a corporate brochure.

Photo-led sections

Larger photos, softer edges and more focus on people and spaces.

Style 04 – Professional & trust-focused

Clear structure

Logical sections answering who you are, what you do, where you are and how to get in touch.

Proof & reassurance

Testimonials, FAQs and guarantee-style messaging to remove doubts.
