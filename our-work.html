Style 01

Futuristic glow

Neon accents, soft glows and a clean grid – ideal for barbers, studios and spaces that already feel modern in real life.

Style 02

Eco-friendly & earthy

Soft greens, warm neutrals and room for real-world photos – perfect for cafés, wellness spaces and eco-leaning brands.

Style 03

Vintage & cosy

Retro typography, warm colour palettes and story-driven sections for vintage shops, salons and heritage brands.

Style 04

Minimal & premium

Light, spacious layouts with minimal colour and strong typography for premium service providers and studios.

Style 05

Bold & playful

Strong colours, quirky shapes and punchy text – great for creative studios, content creators and social-first brands.

Style 06

Dark studio

Moody dark backgrounds, spotlight effects and sharp typography for tattoo, piercing and studio-style businesses.

Style 07

Clean trades

Straightforward layouts, bold call-to-actions and clear service blocks for trades, repairs and local services.

Style 08

Social-first

Layouts built around your TikTok / Instagram content, with obvious routes to enquire, book or buy.
