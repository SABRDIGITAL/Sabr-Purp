Examples of how we can build for you

These examples show the kind of structure, clarity and mood we aim for. When we work together, your content, colours and story sit on top of this level of detail.

Barber studio website

A calm, sharp layout that gets straight to: what you do, where you are, prices and how to book a chair.

  • Hero that shows the space properly, not just a logo.
  • Service list and price ranges that are easy to skim.
  • Booking button that always stays obvious on mobile.

Tattoo studio website

A home for your artists, flash sets and rules so serious clients know exactly how you work before they enquire.

  • Artist sections with styles, photos and availability notes.
  • Clear studio rules, deposits and aftercare in one place.
  • Simple enquiry form where people can attach references.

Café / food website

Focused on atmosphere, menu highlights and opening times so people can decide to visit in seconds.

  • Hero that shows the feel of the café, not just a stock photo.
  • Menu overview or “most popular” dishes with prices.
  • Buttons for directions, phone and opening hours.

Trades / services website

Built for electricians, plumbers, decorators and similar who need to look solid, professional and safe to hire.

  • Services split into simple, understandable sections.
  • Areas covered with realistic response times.
  • Before / after photos and reviews in one clean gallery.

Your site won’t be a template pick. We’ll design around your story, goals and budget.
