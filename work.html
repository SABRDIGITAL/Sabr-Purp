Examples of how we can build for you

These examples show the level of clarity and detail we aim for. When we work together, your content, photos and brand sit on top of this standard.

Barber studio website

Focused on what you do, where you are, prices and how to book a chair – without feeling cluttered.

  • Hero that shows the actual space, not stock photos.
  • Simple price and service list people can skim quickly.
  • Booking button that stays obvious on mobile.

Tattoo studio website

Built around your artists, flash sets and studio rules so serious clients know exactly how you work.

  • Artist sections with styles, examples and availability notes.
  • Studio rules, deposits and aftercare all in one place.
  • Enquiry form where people can attach references.

Café / food website

Built to show the feel of the space, the menu and when you’re open so people can decide quickly to visit.

  • Hero that actually feels like your café, not generic stock.
  • Menu highlights and best-sellers with clear prices.
  • Buttons for directions, hours and booking (if needed).

Trades & services website

For electricians, plumbers, decorators and other trades who want to look professional and safe to hire.

  • Services split into simple, understandable sections.
  • Areas covered with realistic response expectations.
  • Before / after work photos and reviews in one gallery.

You don’t have to pick a layout. We design around your story, your work and what you want the site to do.
