Examples of how we could build for you

These example layouts are here to show the kind of standard you can expect – not templates we force you into. When we work together, your site is designed around your branding, photos and goals.

All images are temporary stock placeholders. Real client projects will replace them as they go live.

Before & after: turning “just a page” into a proper site

Drag the line to see the difference between a basic, text-heavy page and a more considered layout with better hierarchy, imagery and calls-to-action.

Barber studio layout

Sharp hero with prices, service list, photo gallery and clear “Book a chair” call-to-action that works on mobile first.

  • • Hero highlights what you do and where you are.
  • • Services, price ranges and opening times at a glance.
  • • Gallery panel that feels like a curated Instagram grid.

Tattoo studio layout

Artist sections, flash gallery, studio rules and enquiry forms that help you filter for serious clients and manage expectations upfront.

  • • Each artist gets their own mini portfolio section.
  • • FAQs cover deposits, booking rules and aftercare.
  • • Simple form so clients can send references in one place.

Café / food layout

Menu highlights, photos that show the atmosphere, opening hours and location – all easy to skim so people can decide to visit quickly.

  • • Menu or “best sellers” section front and centre.
  • • Photo panels that capture the feel of the place.
  • • Quick-access buttons for directions and contact.

Local trades layout

Service overview, areas covered, testimonials and proof photos so people feel safe letting you into their home or project.

  • • Services broken down into clear blocks.
  • • “Areas we cover” and response time listed plainly.
  • • Real work photos and reviews to back you up.

Beauty / nails layout

Treatment menus, availability hints and soft imagery that make booking feel easy, not awkward.

  • • Treatment list with simple explanations.
  • • Gallery of finished looks with subtle overlays.
  • • Call-to-action tuned for bookings or messages.

Other businesses

Whether you’re running a studio, online service or something more unusual, we can adapt this style to fit.

  • • Flexible layouts that work beyond just local services.
  • • Focus on clarity and trust, no matter the niche.
  • • Future-proof structure for when you grow.

These are just starting points. When we work together, we’ll build something that fits your brand instead of trying to squeeze you into a rigid template.
