Examples of how we could build for you

These are example layouts to show the standard you can expect: clean, trust-focused, easy to skim. When we work together, your site will be built around your own branding, photos and goals.

All imagery shown here is stock for now. Real client work will replace these examples as sites go live.

Barber studio layout

Hero that instantly shows services, vibe and where you are. Clear “Book a chair” button on mobile and desktop.

  • • Price ranges and opening times in one glance.
  • • Gallery grid that feels like a curated Instagram feed.
  • • Map and contact details that are impossible to miss.

Tattoo studio layout

Artists, flash sets, rules and enquiries all in one place so serious clients know exactly how you work.

  • • Artist sections with photos and styles.
  • • Clear booking rules and deposit info.
  • • Simple form where clients can attach references.

Café / food layout

Show the atmosphere, menu highlights and opening hours clearly so people can decide to visit quickly.

  • • Hero that shows your space properly.
  • • Menu or “best sellers” panel that’s easy to skim.
  • • Quick buttons for directions and contact.

Trades / services layout

Clear services, areas covered, proof photos and reviews so people feel safe hiring you.

  • • Services broken into simple blocks.
  • • “Areas we cover” with realistic response times.
  • • Before/after photos and testimonials.

These are just starting points. Your version will be built around your story, your photos and how you like to work with clients.
