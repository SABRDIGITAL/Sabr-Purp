FAQ

Questions people usually ask us

If you’re unsure about anything, you can always just message us. We prefer clear questions and honest answers over confusing jargon.

How long does a website take? + −
Once we have your photos and key information, most small business sites are ready to go live in around 3–7 days. Bigger sites or slower feedback can take a little longer, but we’ll always be clear with you.
Do I need to write all the text myself? + −
No. If you can send us bullet points, rough notes or voice notes, we’ll turn that into clear website copy in a tone that fits your business. You always get to review and approve it.
What do you need from me to get started? + −
We’ll ask for your logo (if you have one), any photos you like, basic business info (services, prices, opening hours, contact details) and any sites you already like the look of.
Can you update the site for me later on? + −
Yes. If you’d like ongoing support, we can handle updates such as new photos, price changes, offers and small layout tweaks, so you don’t need to touch the tech side at all.
What happens if I don’t like the first version? + −
That’s normal. The first version is there so we have something real to react to. We’ll collect your feedback and refine the layout, text and colours until it feels right.
Can you connect my domain and existing emails? + −
Yes. We’ll help connect your domain and keep your existing email addresses working. We’ll also show you how to manage things in plain language.
Will the website work properly on phones? + −
Yes – mobile is our baseline. We design and test on phones first so it loads fast, looks sharp and is easy to use with one hand.
Can you link my Instagram, TikTok or booking apps? + −
Definitely. We can build clear paths from your social media to your website, and then into whatever you use to take bookings or enquiries.
Do I need to pay for hosting or anything extra? + −
We’ll walk you through the simple ongoing costs (like domain and hosting) in plain English. No hidden surprises, just what you actually need.
What if I’ve never had a website before? + −
That’s completely fine. A lot of our favourite projects start from scratch. We’ll keep things simple, explain each step and handle the technical bits for you.

Still not sure? You can always send us a message in normal language.
