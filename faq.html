Questions you might be thinking

Being unsure about getting a website made is normal. Here are clear, direct answers in plain English.

“We’re small – is a website actually worth it?” +

Yes. Especially for local service businesses. People search your name, see a link and expect something more solid than just a social account. A clean site instantly makes you feel more established than others who only rely on Instagram or TikTok.

“I’m not good with tech. Will I be able to manage it?” +

We keep the setup simple and give you a short handover guide. If you’d rather not touch it at all, we can look after updates so you focus on your actual work.

“Do we need anything in place before we start?” +

A business name, a rough list of services and a couple of websites or brands you like is enough to start. If you already have a domain, great. If not, we can help you choose and set one up.

“How long does it usually take?” +

Once we’ve agreed the layout and content, a typical small-business site usually takes days, not months. The main variable is how quickly we both approve content and visuals.

“How do you approach pricing?” +

We keep pricing straightforward and talk about it after the discovery chat, when we actually know what you need. No random add-ons dropped on you later.

“What if I don’t like the design?” +

You’ll see a working preview before anything goes live. We’d rather refine it with you than leave you with something you’re not happy to show customers.

Still unsure? Tell us where you’re at and we’ll tell you honestly what we’d do in your position.
