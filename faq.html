FAQ

Questions people usually ask us

If you don’t see your question here, you can always message us directly – we’d rather you ask than guess.

How much does a website cost? + −
For now, our pricing is simple:
· £250 for a full website build from scratch
· £200 for a remap of an existing website
· £75 per month if you want us to handle ongoing updates and upkeep
How long does it take? + −
Once we have your photos and key info, most small business sites take around 1–3 weeks. If you’re quick to reply with feedback, it can be even faster.
Do I need to write all the text myself? + −
No. If you can give us bullet points and basic details, we can write the website copy in a tone that matches your business. You’ll always get to approve it.
What do you need from me to start? + −
A quick chat, your logo (if you have one), any photos you like, and your core business info: services, pricing, opening hours, and contact details.
Can you update the site for me later? + −
Yes. If you choose the £75/month upkeep option, we’ll handle ongoing changes like new photos, price updates, offers and small layout tweaks.

Still unsure? We’re happy to talk things through in normal language.
