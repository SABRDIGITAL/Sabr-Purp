FAQ

Questions people usually ask us

Straight answers in normal language. If you don’t see your question here, you can always message us – we’d rather you ask than guess.

How long does a website take? + −
Once we have your photos and clear information, most small business sites are ready to go live in 3–7 days. Bigger sites or slower feedback can take a little longer, but we’ll always be clear about timings so you’re not left guessing.
Do I need to write all the text myself? + −
No. If you can send us bullet points, rough notes or even voice notes, we’ll turn that into clear website copy in a tone that fits your business. You always get to review and approve everything before it goes live.
What do you need from me to get started? + −
We’ll ask for your logo (if you have one), any photos you like, and basic business info: services, pricing, opening hours, contact details and any websites you already like the look of. We’ll guide you through it step by step.
Can you update the site for me later? + −
Yes. If you’d like ongoing support, we can handle updates such as new photos, price changes, offers and small layout tweaks. We can also agree a simple monthly arrangement if you prefer not to touch the tech at all.
What happens if I don’t like the first version? + −
That’s normal. The first version is there so you’ve got something real to react to. We’ll collect your feedback, ask what feels “off” and then refine the design and wording until it actually feels like your business.
Can you connect my domain and existing email? + −
Yes. We’ll help connect your domain (website address) and make sure any existing email addresses keep working. We’ll also show you, in plain language, how to manage basics in future if you’d like.
Will my site work properly on phones? + −
Yes – mobile is the baseline we design for, not an afterthought. We constantly check layouts, buttons and text on smaller screens so people can call, book or message you easily from their phone.
Do I need to pay for hosting or anything else? + −
Most sites need three things: your domain name, hosting and sometimes email. We’ll walk you through the options and keep things as simple and affordable as possible. We’ll never push add-ons you don’t actually need.
Can you work with my existing branding and photos? + −
Definitely. If you already have a logo, colour palette or photo style, we’ll build around it so everything feels consistent. If you’re not sure where to start, we can suggest a direction that matches your space and audience.
How do we actually start working together? + −
Start by sending us a quick message with what you do and what you’d like your website to change. From there, we’ll suggest a plan, give you a clear price, and if it feels right we’ll book in a start date and get moving.

Still unsure? Tell us what you do and we’ll explain what we’d recommend in plain English.
