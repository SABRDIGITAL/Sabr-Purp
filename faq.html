Questions you might be thinking

If you’re unsure or nervous about getting a website made, that’s completely normal. Here are clear answers in plain English.

“We’re small – is a website actually worth it?” +

Yes. Especially for local service businesses. People search your name, see a link and expect something more solid than just a social profile. A clean, modern site instantly makes you feel more established than competitors who only rely on platforms they don’t control.

“I’m not good with tech. Will I be able to manage it?” +

We keep things as simple as possible and give you a basic handover guide. If you’d rather not touch it at all, we can look after updates so you can focus on your actual work.

“Do we need anything in place before we start?” +

Not much. A business name, rough list of services and maybe a few reference websites or Instagram accounts you like is enough to start. If you already have a domain, great – if not, we can guide you.

“How long does it usually take?” +

It depends on how complex the site is and how quickly content is agreed. Once we have what we need, we’re usually talking days, not months, for a normal small-business site.

“How do you approach pricing?” +

We keep pricing straightforward and talk about it openly once we understand what you actually need. No surprise extras stacked on afterwards – the first chat is about fit and scope, not pressure.

“What if I don’t like the design?” +

You’ll see the site and be able to give feedback before it goes live. We’d rather refine it with you than leave you with something you’re not proud to show people.

Still not sure if now is the right time? You’re welcome to reach out, tell us where you’re at, and we’ll tell you honestly what we’d do in your position.
