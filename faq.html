FAQ

Questions people usually ask us

These are the questions we get most often. If you don’t see yours here, message us directly – we’d rather you ask than guess.

How long does a website take? + −
For most small-business websites, we say around 3–7 days once we’ve got your photos and key info. If you’re quick with feedback, it can be sooner. Bigger or more complex sites can take longer, but we’ll always tell you upfront.
Do I need to write all the text myself? + −
No. If you can give us bullet points and basic details, we’ll write the website copy in a tone that matches your business. You’ll always get to read and approve everything before it goes live.
What do you need from me to start? + −
A quick chat (or you can fill in our questionnaire), your logo if you have one, your best photos, and your key info: services, opening times, contact details and any reviews you love.
Can you update the site for me later? + −
Yes. We offer an ongoing upkeep option where we handle changes like new photos, price updates, offers and small layout tweaks. If you’d rather do it yourself, we can record a short video showing you how to edit key bits in plain English.
Do I have to pay everything upfront? + −
We usually split things into stages – a deposit to secure the project, then the rest when the site is ready to go live. We’ll explain the exact breakdown clearly before we start.
What if I already have a website? + −
That’s totally fine. We can either tidy up what you’ve got or rebuild from scratch using your existing content as a starting point. We’ll also help with moving your domain if needed.
Will my site work properly on phones? + −
Yes. Mobile is the priority. We design everything so it feels smooth and easy to use on phones first, then scale it up nicely for tablets and desktops.
Can you help with photos and content if I don’t have much? + −
We can work with what you’ve got – even if that’s just phone photos and rough notes. We’ll help pick the best images, suggest what else would be useful, and write the copy so it all fits together.
What platforms do you build on? + −
We choose the tech based on what’s best for you – not what’s fanciest. That might be a custom build, a simple site you can edit yourself, or a mix. The goal is always: fast, reliable and easy to update.
What happens after the site goes live? + −
We’ll walk you through the site, check everything is working, and agree what happens next: either we keep looking after it for you, or you take over with us in the background when you need changes.

Still unsure? We’re happy to talk things through in normal language.
